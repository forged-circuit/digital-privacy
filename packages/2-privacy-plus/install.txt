# 🔵 Privacy Plus – Installation Guide

Privacy Plus is the recommended full home protection setup.

It improves Starter Shield by adding stronger filtering, better device coverage, and more consistent network protection.

This guide is designed to be simple and step-by-step.

---

# 🧭 Before You Begin

You will need:

* Access to your router settings
* A device connected to your Wi-Fi
* About 15–20 minutes
* Starter Shield is NOT required (this can be installed fresh)

---

# ⚙️ Step 1: Access Your Router

* Open a web browser
* Enter your router address (usually `192.168.1.1` or `192.168.0.1`)
* Log in with your admin username/password

---

# ⚙️ Step 2: Set Network Protection (DNS)

Privacy Plus works by directing your internet traffic through a protected filtering system.

You will update your DNS settings:

* Go to “Internet” or “Network Settings”
* Find “DNS Settings”
* Replace existing DNS with your Privacy Plus configuration

Save your changes.

---

# ⚙️ Step 3: Restart Your Network

To apply changes:

* Restart your router
* Wait 1–2 minutes for internet to reconnect

---

# ⚙️ Step 4: Reconnect Your Devices

Once your network is back online:

* Reconnect phones, laptops, TVs, and smart devices
* Or restart devices if they do not connect automatically

Privacy Plus will now apply across all devices.

---

# 🧪 Step 5: Confirm Protection is Active

Open a browser and visit normal websites.

You should notice:

* Fewer ads
* Faster loading pages
* Reduced tracking content
* Cleaner browsing experience

---

# 🧠 What Privacy Plus Adds

Compared to Starter Shield, you now get:

* Stronger filtering rules
* Better coverage across devices
* More consistent blocking behavior
* Improved stability for smart home devices

---

# ⚠️ Important Notes

* Some ads may still appear inside apps (this is normal)
* Certain streaming services may behave slightly differently
* Changes may take a few minutes to fully apply

If something seems off, restarting your router usually fixes it.

---

# 🧭 When to Use Privacy Plus

Use this if you want:

* Stronger protection than basic setup
* Better control over network privacy
* A stable “set it and forget it” system

---

# 🚀 What’s Next?

Once Privacy Plus is installed, you can:

* Keep it as-is (most users do)
* Upgrade to Business Essentials for more control
* Or explore advanced protection packages

👉 View all options in `/packages/`

---

# 🧠 Summary

Privacy Plus gives you:

* Full home network protection
* Stronger ad and tracker blocking
* Simple setup process
* Reliable everyday performance
