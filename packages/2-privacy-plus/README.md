# 🔵 Privacy Plus (Recommended)

Full home privacy system.

This package combines network protection with device awareness and password security.

---

# 🧭 What This Package Does

* Blocks ads and trackers across all devices
* Monitors your network activity
* Secures your credentials

---

# 🧩 What’s Included

* Pi-hole (DNS filtering)
* Home Assistant (device monitoring)
* Vaultwarden (password manager)

---

# 👤 Who This Is For

* Most users
* Families
* Users who want complete home protection

---

# ⚙️ What You’ll Need

* Device for Pi-hole
* Device for Home Assistant
* Device for Vaultwarden (can be shared)

---

# 🎯 Outcome

After setup:

* All devices are protected
* You can see what’s happening on your network
* Your passwords are secure and centralized
