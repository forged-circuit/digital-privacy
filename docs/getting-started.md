# 🚀 Getting Started

Welcome to the Digital Privacy system.

This guide is the simplest way to begin using the system without needing technical experience.

If you are unsure where to start, follow this page step-by-step.

---

# 🧭 Step 1: Understand What This Is

Digital Privacy helps you:

* Block ads and trackers on your entire network
* Secure passwords and accounts
* Protect your home or small business network
* Monitor and control internet traffic

Everything works at the network level (your router), not individual apps.

---

# 🧭 Step 2: Choose Your Starting Path

Pick the type of user you are:

---

## 🏠 Home Users

If this is your home network:

👉 Go to: `/protection-plans/home-users.md`

---

## 🔧 Intermediate / Advanced Users

If you are comfortable with slightly more control:

👉 Go to: `/protection-plans/pro-users.md`

---

## 🏢 Small Business (≤10 users)

If you are setting up an office or business network:

👉 Go to: `/protection-plans/business.md`

---

# 📦 Step 3: Choose a Package

After selecting your path:

👉 Go to: `/packages/`

You will see all available protection levels:

* Starter Shield
* Privacy Plus
* Business Essentials
* Pro Business Guard
* Ultimate Offense

Each package includes step-by-step installation instructions.

---

# ⚙️ Step 4: Install Your System

Each package contains an `install.md` file.

Follow it step-by-step:

* No prior networking knowledge required
* No coding required
* Just follow instructions in order

---

# 🧠 Important Concept

You are not installing separate apps.

You are configuring your **entire network** to be protected.

---

# ⚠️ Before You Begin

Make sure you have:

* Access to your router admin panel
* A computer or laptop for setup
* 15–120 minutes depending on package
* Stable internet connection

---

# 🧭 If You Get Stuck

If something does not work:

* Restart your router
* Re-check your steps in the install guide
* Try again slowly step-by-step

Most issues come from skipped setup steps.

---

# 🧠 Summary

Your journey is:

1. Choose your user type
2. Pick a protection plan
3. Follow the install guide
4. Your network becomes protected
