# 📡 Step 3: Assign Devices or VLANs

---

# 🧭 What You Are Doing

You will ensure your devices are using Pi-hole for DNS.

---

# 🏠 Option A: Standard Home Setup

* Set your router DNS to the Pi-hole IP
* Restart devices
* All devices will automatically use filtering

---

# 📺 Option B: Smart TVs / IoT Devices

* Place TVs on a separate network (optional)
* Connect them to your Wi-Fi
* Pi-hole will apply filtering rules

---

# 🧠 Important

* VLANs are configured on your router (not in Pi-hole)
* Pi-hole handles filtering centrally

---

# ✅ Expected Result

* Devices begin using Pi-hole automatically
* Filtering applies without per-device setup

---

# 🎉 Setup Complete

Your Pi-hole is now fully configured and active.
